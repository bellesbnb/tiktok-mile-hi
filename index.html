          07 ·  before you post

          predict your virality.

          Drop your edited clip into the Higgsfield Virality Predictor before you post. The model scores your hook, hold rate, and ceiling so you fix what's weak instead of guessing.

          OPEN VIRALITY PREDICTOR
          how to use it
          1. 01Export your CapCut edit (1080p, 9:16, MP4)
          2. 02Upload it to higgsfield.ai/apps/virality-predictor
          3. 03Wait ~30s for the AI to score it
          4. 04Read the breakdown (hook, hold, payoff)
          5. 05Fix the weakest part. Re-run. Post when it's green.
          score below 60? fix these first
          • →Cut the first 1.5 seconds. The hook starts later than you think.
          • →Open with a visual change, not a face. Movement = retention.
          • →Caption must be readable in 1 second. Bigger, bolder, centered.
          • →Use trending audio. Search the trend's sound from the trend card above.
          • →Cut anything over 12s unless the payoff justifies it.
          • →End on a loop — the last frame should make people watch again.
          the rule

          If your video scores below 50, don't post it. Reshoot, re-edit, or pick a different trend. Posting a flop hurts your account more than not posting at all.
